SHIELD — Privacy Policy

Because safety should not wait.

Last updated: April 2026 | Effective immediately

Summary: SHIELD collects only what is needed to keep you safe. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. You own your data and can delete it anytime.

1. Who We Are

SHIELD is a women's personal safety application built by Sobhana Kumari, Sanskriti Tyagi, Vaidehi Gupta & Jay Tyagi. Contact us at: kumarisobhana119@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and phone number when you register.
  • GPS location: Collected only when an SOS is triggered, or when you enable Live Location Sharing. Not collected in the background without your explicit activation.
  • Trusted contacts: Phone numbers you add as emergency contacts, stored encrypted.
  • SOS history: Timestamps and location snapshots of past SOS events for your review only.
  • Audio/Video recordings: Only captured during an active SOS event. Stored in your secure cloud storage folder, accessible only to you.
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous, aggregated data (e.g., feature usage counts) to improve the app. No personal identifiers linked.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To send SOS alerts with your live GPS location to your trusted contacts during emergencies.
  • To store your emergency contact numbers for quick alert dispatch.
  • To securely preserve audio/video evidence recordings during emergencies.
  • To send you push notifications for check-in reminders and safety alerts.
  • To improve app performance and features using anonymized analytics.

4. Device Permissions Explained

  • LOCATION (Fine + Background): Required to share your real-time GPS coordinates with trusted contacts when SOS is triggered. Background access allows the location to update even when the app is not on screen during an active emergency.
  • CAMERA: Used exclusively to begin silent video recording during an SOS event as evidence. Not used for any other purpose.
  • MICROPHONE: Used for (a) voice command trigger ("SHIELD HELP") and (b) audio recording during active SOS. Not used for continuous background listening.
  • SMS: Used to send emergency SMS alerts to your trusted contacts when internet is unavailable (offline fallback). SHIELD never reads your existing SMS messages.
  • CONTACTS: Used only to help you select trusted contacts from your phonebook. We do not upload or read your full contacts list.
  • ACCELEROMETER (Motion Sensor): Used to detect shake gestures (3 shakes = SOS trigger). Runs as a lightweight background service.

5. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data to any third party, ever.
  • We do not share your data with advertisers.
  • Your GPS location is shared only with the trusted contacts you have designated.
  • We may use trusted third-party services (Firebase by Google for database/storage, SMS gateway for alerts) bound by their own privacy policies. These services do not have access to your data beyond what is needed for the service.
  • We may disclose information if required by Indian law or a valid court order.

6. Data Retention

  • SOS location data is retained for a maximum of 30 days, then automatically deleted.
  • Evidence recordings are stored until you delete them.
  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • You can request full data deletion at any time by emailing us.

7. Your Rights

  • Access all data SHIELD holds about you.
  • Correct or update your personal information at any time.
  • Delete your account and all associated data permanently.
  • Withdraw any permission you have granted (via your phone's Settings).
  • Export your SOS history and evidence recordings.

8. Children & Minor Users

SHIELD is designed for users of all ages. If a user is under 18, we recommend that a parent or guardian assist with account setup and act as a trusted contact. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted storage, secure HTTPS connections, and JWT-based authentication. Trusted contact numbers are stored encrypted. Evidence recordings are stored in your personal, access-controlled storage folder.

10. Emergency Disclaimer

⚠️ SHIELD is a support and alert tool. It is not a replacement for contacting official emergency services. Always call 112 (India) in a life-threatening emergency. SHIELD is designed to complement, not replace, official emergency response.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by email. Continued use of SHIELD after changes constitutes acceptance.
